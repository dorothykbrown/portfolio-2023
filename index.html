About Me

Hi, I'm Dorothy!

I am a software engineer with 3+ years of experience in developing and delivering high-quality, scalable software solutions for the FinTech, Advertising and Automobile industries. I am proficient in Python and have additional experience in Golang, Ruby, React and JavaScript, with expertise in developing RESTful APIs, microservices, and cloud-based applications. I led the development of multiple projects, including a reporting system to provide users with real-time insights into their ad performance on Bing, Facebook and Google, resulting in improved customer experience and increased revenue.

I hold a Master of Science from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Civil Engineering and Sustainability and previously worked as a project engineer on large and small-scale projects around the greater New York City area. Additionally, I was awarded a fellowship from the Fulbright U.S. Student Program to conduct research in the Pontificia Universidade Catolica in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil on the use of natural materials in sustainable contruction.
